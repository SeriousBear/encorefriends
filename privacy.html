Privacy Policy

I built Encore because I'm a fellow concert-goer who's constantly distracted — always the last to know about the show, always finding out my friends went without me, always missing the presale by five minutes. This app exists to fix that. Protecting your privacy is part of the deal.

This policy explains what information Encore Friends collects, why we collect it, and how we use it. We will never sell your data, show you ads based on it, or share it with anyone who doesn't need it to make the app work.

01
What We Collect

When you sign in with Google, we receive your name and email address from Google. This is used to create your Encore profile and identify you within the app.

Information you choose to add to your profile:

  • Display name and handle
  • Location (city)
  • Bio / quote
  • Favorite genres and artists
  • Bucket list artists
  • Social links (Instagram, Spotify, SoundCloud)
  • Lifetime show count

Concert data we extract from your Gmail when you choose to run a scan:

  • Artist / performer name
  • Venue and city
  • Show date
  • Ticket source (e.g. Ticketmaster, Resident Advisor, Eventbrite)
02
Gmail Access
⚡ The short version: we scan your inbox for ticket confirmation emails only. We extract the show details (artist, venue, date). We do not read, store, or transmit the content of any other emails. Ever.

When you click "Scan Gmail," Encore requests read-only access to your Gmail account via Google's OAuth system. We use this access to search for emails that look like concert ticket confirmations — things like order confirmations from Ticketmaster, Resident Advisor, Eventbrite, AXS, SeatGeek, and similar services.

We look at the subject line, sender, and snippet of matching emails only. Non-matching emails are never accessed. The email content itself is processed by an AI model to extract the concert details and is not stored.

Your Gmail access token is never stored on our servers. It lives in your browser session only and expires when you close the app or revoke access.

You can revoke Gmail access at any time at myaccount.google.com/permissions.

03
How We Use Your Data

Your data is used only to make Encore work for you:

  • To show your upcoming shows in your feed
  • To let friends see what shows you're going to (only people you follow and who follow you)
  • To surface mutual shows between you and your friends
  • To power genre and artist discovery features
  • To remember your profile settings between sessions

We do not use your data for advertising. We do not sell your data. We do not share your data with third parties except the infrastructure providers listed below who are necessary to run the app.

04
Third-Party Services

Encore uses the following third-party services to operate:

  • Supabase — our database and authentication provider. Your profile and concert data is stored here. Privacy policy ↗
  • Google OAuth — used for sign-in and Gmail scanning. Privacy policy ↗
  • Anthropic Claude — used to parse ticket confirmation emails and extract concert details. Email snippets are sent to Anthropic's API for processing and are not retained. Privacy policy ↗
  • Netlify — our hosting provider. Privacy policy ↗
05
Data Retention & Deletion

Your profile and concert data is stored in our database until you delete your account. To request deletion of all your data, email us at privacy@encorefriends.com and we will remove everything within 30 days.

Gmail access tokens are session-only and are never stored on our servers.

06
Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate profile data (directly in the app via Edit Profile)
  • Request deletion of all your data
  • Revoke Gmail access at any time via your Google account settings
  • Export your concert data (coming soon)

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@encorefriends.com.

07
Children's Privacy

Encore is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, contact us and we will delete it promptly.

08
Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy as the app grows. When we do, we'll update the date at the top of this page. For significant changes we'll notify users via the app.

09
